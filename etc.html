general rules Participants must carry their College ID cards with them. On-spot Registration is not allowed. Registration fees are not refundable. Misbehavior or indiscipline will lead to disqualification. The use of state-altering substances is strictly prohibited. Participants are requested to bring their own gadgets. No gadgets will be provided by organizing team at the day of the event. Instructions for each event will be given by the respective coordinator before the event. As all events are conducted in campus, participants presence in the campus is must. Judges' decision will be final. College will not be responsible for any loss of valuables. Students must register to events according to event schedule and no students are allowed to register in multiple simultaneous scheduled events. The organizing team will not be responsible if the participant is unable to follow the time schedule.

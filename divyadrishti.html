About the Event

extraordinary journey where the magic of 'Divya Drishti' guides your lens to new horizons. This captivating event, part of our college department fest, dares you to capture the unseen, transforming the everyday into the extraordinary. Step beyond the surface and unveil the fest's hidden stories through your unique perspective. In this visionary contest, the boundaries of photography are yours to redefine. Whether you experiment with framing, play with light and shadows, or master the art of perspective, your creativity knows no limits. Compete for coveted prizes, collaborate with fellow dreamers, and let your camera reveal the fest's soul.

Rules
  • Individual participants
  • There will be 3 rounds (including Final round).
  • Participants are required to bring their own card reader and USB cables and other necessary accessories.
  • Participants are allowed to use smartphone and Camera for capturing photographs.
  • Topic will be provided on spot.
  • Participants must click photos/videos within given time and submit.
  • Editing software is not allowed.
  • The judgment will be based on Exposure, Focus, Framing, Perspective, and Adherence to Theme.
Prizes
  • 1st Prize: ₹2,000
  • 2nd Prize: ₹1,500
  • Certificates for all participants.

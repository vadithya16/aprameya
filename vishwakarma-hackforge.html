Event Details



About the Event

Vishwakarma HackForge is a premier hackathon that brings together bright minds to create impactful and innovative solutions. Designed for tech enthusiasts, engineers, and problem solvers, this event encourages participants to push the boundaries of creativity and technology. Over two exciting rounds, teams will present their groundbreaking ideas and then implement them in 24 hours, competing for recognition and incredible prizes. Whether you’re an aspiring developer, designer, or entrepreneur, Vishwakarma HackForge provides a platform to showcase your skills, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and make a difference through technology

Rules and Guidelines
  • Team Composition
    • Maximum of 4 members per team.
  • Event Structure
    • Round 1:
      Idea Presentation (Online Submission) (By 3rd February)
    • Round 2:
      Implementation (Offline - 24 hours) (6th February, 4:00 PM - 7th February, 4:00 PM)
  • General Rules
    • Teams must bring their own laptops and IoT components.
    • Wi-Fi and extension boxes will be provided.
    • AI tools are permitted; however, the project must be original and demonstrate innovative problem-solving.
    • Replicating existing projects is strictly prohibited. Plagiarism checks will be conducted, and teams found guilty will be disqualified.
Prizes
  • 1st Prize: ₹10,000
  • 2nd Prize: ₹5,000
  • Gift Voucher from Interview Buddy

Tracks

Open Innovation

Open innovation encourages collaboration with external partners to accelerate innovation. It combines inbound and outbound strategies to reduce R&D costs and create diverse solutions, despite challenges like IP concerns.

Generative AI

Unleash your imagination with Generative AI, exploring creative technological solutions and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in artificial intelligence.

Internet of Things (IoT)

Discover the world of IoT, where smart devices revolutionize industries, homes, and healthcare by creating a more connected and efficient environment.

Game Development

Dive into game development and craft virtual universes, interactive gameplay, and immersive experiences with coding, AI, and graphics.

Web3 & Blockchain

Explore decentralized technology with Web3 & Blockchain. Learn how dApps, smart contracts, and secure transactions are shaping the future.

About the Event

IT Sarvagyaani: The Ultimate Battle of Tech Brilliance! Get ready to immerse yourself in an exciting competition where your tech knowledge and problem-solving skills are put to the test. As part of our college department fest, IT Sarvagyaani challenges you to explore, innovate, and conquer a series of unique rounds designed to bring out the best in every participant. From cracking intricate puzzles and identifying key technologies to solving code errors and bidding your way to victory, this event is crafted to push your boundaries. Whether you're a tech enthusiast or an aspiring IT pro, this is your moment to shine. Compete for top honors, collaborate with like-minded peers, and prove your mastery of the ever-evolving tech world!

Rules
  • Each team consists of 2 participants
  • The event consists of 4 rounds spread over 2 days.
  • Each round tests knowledge of programming, networks, databases, logic, and current tech trends.
  • No internet access is allowed during the event.
  • Correct answers will earn points, while incorrect answers will receive no points
  • Elimination happens after Rounds 2 and 3, cutting 50% of teams.
  • The top 5 teams from Day 1 will qualify for the Final Round on Day 2
Prizes
  • 1st Prize: ₹2,000
  • 2nd Prize: ₹1,500
  • Certificates for all participants.

About the Event

Attention, Mythology Enthusiasts!
Get ready to embark on a legendary adventure with CHAKRANIDHI—a mythological treasure hunt like no other! Gather your partner, solve mind-bending clues, uncover hidden treasures, and unlock the secrets of ancient myths. Challenge your intellect, creativity, and teamwork across three exciting rounds and compete for the title of ultimate treasure hunters!
Are you up for the challenge?
Join us at Chakranidhi for an unforgettable mythological journey! No gadgets, just brains and teamwork! Let's see who can decode the myths and claim victory. See you there!

Rules
  • Each team consists of 2 participants.
  • Solve mythological clues and challenges, including riddles, puzzles, and tasks throughout the hunt.
  • Electronic devices are not allowed unless explicitly instructed by the event coordinators.
  • Follow the clues in order, some of which will lead to new locations or challenges.
  • Round 2 and Round 3 will have time limits. Be quick and efficient, but don’t rush too much!
  • In Round 1, there is no elimination, so all teams will continue to Round 2.
  • Round 1 and 2 will be conducted on day 1.
  • Round 3 on Day 2 will feature the toughest challenges, requiring sharp thinking and strategy.
  • Collaboration is essential, and both team members should contribute equally tosolving the clues
  • The team that completes all clues within the time limit in Round 2 and Round 3 will win.
Prizes
  • 1st Prize: ₹2,000
  • 2nd Prize: ₹1,500
  • Certificates for all participants.

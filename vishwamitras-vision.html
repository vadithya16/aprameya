Vishwamitra’s vision

Strategize, Lead, Excel: Master the Art of IT Management!

Event Details



About the Event

The "Vishwamitra’s vision" event challenges participants to showcase their skills in strategic decisionmaking, project management, and leadership within the realm of IT. Contestants will be presented with dynamic scenarios where they must efficiently allocate resources, manage teams, and prioritize tasks to achieve specific technology-driven goals. The event emphasizes the importance of effective communication, adaptability, and problem-solving in managing IT projects, with outcomes including enhanced organizational and leadership skills, as well as the ability to navigate the complexities of modern tech environments. Participants will leave with a deeper understanding of IT management practices, preparing them for future roles in the field.

Rules
  • This is an individual event. Each participant must compete alone
  • The competition consists of five rounds, which will be spread across two days.
  • Participants are strictly prohibited from using any gadgets or electronic devices during the event unless instructed
  • Each round will evaluate participants' knowledge in technology, aptitude, and situational analysis.
  • Points will be awarded for each correct answer. No points will be awarded for incorrect answers.
  • Please Carry Your Own Writing Pads (Mandatory)
  • The elimination process begins from Round 2, and participants must perform consistently to remain in the competition.
  • At the conclusion of Day 1, the top 4 performers will be shortlisted and advanced to the Final Round on Day 2.
Prizes
  • 1st Prize: ₹4,000
  • Certificates for all participants.

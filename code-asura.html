About the Event

Welcome to Code Asura: The Duo Code Conquest! Ready to conquer the coding realm with a teammate by your side? This thrilling event at our college IT fest welcomes pairs of coders to join forces and face an array of challenging programming tasks. Embark on this exhilarating adventure where each team of two will strategize, code, and solve intricate problems. It’s not just about individual skill, but the power of collaboration and synergy. Together, you'll navigate through complex algorithms, optimize your solutions, and rise above the competition. Join us for this mythological coding challenge and prove that two minds are stronger than one in the epic quest of Code Asura.

Rules
  • Each team consists of 2 members
  • The event comprises three rounds.
  • Details and instructions for each round will be provided on the day of the event.
  • Rounds 1 and 2 will be conducted on Day 1.
  • Round 3 will be conducted on Day 2.
  • There will be no eliminations in the first round
  • Participants may code in C, C++, or Java.
  • Participants should have a solid understanding of Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA), Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) concepts, Networking, Operating System(OS), Database Management Systems (DBMS), and related subjects.
  • Participants are not allowed to use any kind of electronic devices during the event.
Prizes
  • 1st Prize: ₹2,000
  • 2nd Prize: ₹1,500
  • Certificates for all participants.
